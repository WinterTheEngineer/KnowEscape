Key Offerings

Work one-on-one to clarify your business model, revenue streams, and go-to-market strategy. We help you turn a rough idea into a practical plan that investors, funders, and partners can understand and support.

Understand your target customers, competitors, and pricing so you can launch with confidence. We guide you through practical validation steps to test demand before you commit serious time and capital.

Gain access to experienced mentors who have built and supported businesses across Gauteng. Regular sessions keep you accountable, help you navigate setbacks, and ensure that your next move is always intentional.

Prepare your startup to qualify for corporate Enterprise & Supplier Development (ESD) opportunities and other funding channels. We help you organise your compliance, documentation, and pitch so B-BBEE-aligned corporates can confidently support your growth.
